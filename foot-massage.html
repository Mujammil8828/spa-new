What is a Foot Massage?

A foot massage is a therapeutic treatment that involves kneading and manipulating the muscles, pressure points, and joints of the feet. While it is incredibly relaxing on its own, it is also a key part of many broader massage therapies like Swedish and Deep Tissue massages.
A professional foot massage goes beyond a simple rub; it uses specific techniques like long strokes, circular pressure, thumb walks, and stretching to release tension not just in the feet, but throughout the entire body.
